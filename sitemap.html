5-Email Nurture Sequence — Day 0 → Day 10

Day 0 — Immediate
01
You are founding member #[N]
Welcome. Confirm their number. What the founding network means. Referral link introduced. One sentence on what's coming.
Day 2
02
The Peeping Tom in your pocket
Link to brochure. Deepen the problem. Make it personal — their data, their profile, their $0 received. Emotional charge.
Day 4
03
You already have 3 Surfaces
Explain the Surface concept using their likely real life. Commute route, skills, free hours. Make it concrete and personal. Remove abstraction.
Day 7
04
Your first Logistic
Walk through one real example end-to-end. Someone posts a Logistic. The Rider matches. It resolves. FavourBucks flow. Click to install.
Day 10
05
The founding network is building. Here's your install.
Hard CTA. Member count update. "The people you referred are already inside." Direct to install.html. Urgency: early nodes earn most.
