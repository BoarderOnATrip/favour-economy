The Mathematics of Early

Why founding members earn more. Forever.

The Favour Economy is built on asymptotic reward logic. Scarcity is rewarded. Abundance is the natural outcome. Both are features, not bugs.

◉

Asymptotic Rewards

When the network is sparse, rewards are highest. As coverage saturates, rewards approach — but never reach — zero. Founding members always earn more than later joiners.

⬡

The Surface Principle

Every person contributes a Surface — a slice of time, skill, capital, and location. Surfaces stack. Early Surfaces shape the network's core topology.

◈

FavourBucks, Not Interest

Earn FavourBucks for every Logistic you complete or asset you contribute. No compound interest. No debt traps. Value flows to work, not to waiting.

◎

1% Builds the Mesh

One percent of every transaction funds the mesh network infrastructure — the internet no one owns, growing from the inside out. Founders own its earliest nodes.

⬭

Group Commons

Pool assets with your group. Share vehicles, storage, equipment. Dividends flow to every member. Founding groups get priority city activation.

◇

Fast or Free

Network slack = free delivery. Network pressure = paid delivery. Founding members get priority routing in saturated markets. You set the speed.
